MIDAS introduces a multi-LLM iterative pipeline for document summarization that adapts to data distribution shifts across document types. By leveraging multiple language models in a structured iterative loop, the system produces more coherent and faithful summaries than single-model baselines, with particular gains on heterogeneous document collections.
