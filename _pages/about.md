I’m a Senior AI Engineer and Project Manager at Volkswagen Innovation Center, leading research at the intersection of generative AI, autonomous systems, and embodied robotics. My current work spans robotic-arm-based in-cabin testing for automotive manufacturing using vision-language-action models and world models, flow matching-based VLA models for autonomous driving, and Multimodal Semantic Search — a project I led from concept to board-level deployment across all Volkswagen brands. I define technical direction, regularly present results to senior leadership, and shape strategy for high-impact innovation efforts.

In previous roles, I’ve developed both deep learning and classical computer vision systems — including GAN-based pipelines for creative applications, zero-shot learning for fine-grained fashion classification, contextual bandit recommender systems, and multi-label video classification for content safety. My work bridges foundational research and rapid prototyping, with a strong focus on solving real-world problems across diverse domains. You can learn more [here](https://www.womentech.net/video/recommender-systems-and-women-shoppers-dhanashree-balaram)


Outside of work, I'm passionate about education - specifically, how different teaching pedagogies can enhance students’ understanding of foundational concepts in math and English. I analyze data from schools in India that emphasize peer-learning environments and am particularly interested in how these approaches impact students across diverse socioeconomic and regional backgrounds.

### Focus Areas

- **Vision-Language-Action Models**  
  Researching flow matching-based generative models within the VLA framework for autonomous driving and robotic-arm testing, enabling smooth, continuous action prediction grounded in visual and language context.

- **World Models for Robotic Automation**  
  Building world models and vision-language systems that enable robots to reason about environments and automate complex in-cabin validation workflows for automotive manufacturing.

- **3D Generative Modeling**  
  Researching novel 3D point-cloud generation architectures using probabilistic diffusion models to automate structural car design — with two patents pending for multimodal guidance and feature-aware sampling.

- **Multimodal Search & Document Intelligence**  
  Developed and deployed Multimodal Semantic Search as a VIP project across all VW brands. Recent work includes MIDAS, a multi-LLM iterative summarization framework accepted to ICDAR 2026.
