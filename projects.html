Promoting Personalized Learning: The Effectiveness of Peer Teaching Pedagogy

In collaboration with Involve, I analyzed data from schools in Bangalore, India, comparing and contrasting peer-to-peer learning strategies against classical/traditional approaches. This project aimed to understand how peer teaching could enhance personalized learning outcomes.

The findings were published in the Proceedings of the International Conference on Technology 4 Education (T4E) 2023, hosted by IIT Bombay. ISBN Number: 978-81-972700-0-0.
