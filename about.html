About Me

In 2015, a single undergraduate class on artificial neural networks changed everything for me. It was the moment I realized exactly what I wanted to do with my career. Combining a solid foundation in hardware engineering with a specialization in machine learning opened up a path to solving real-world industry challenges with innovation and practicality. Today, with over 5 years of industry experience, I’m proud to serve as a Project Lead and AI Engineer at Volkswagen. I mentor emerging AI engineers, guide research directions, and strategize long-term goals for cutting-edge AI projects. My work revolves around bridging theory and application, crafting solutions that navigate real-world constraints while pushing the boundaries of innovation.
