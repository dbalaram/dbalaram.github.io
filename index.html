Hi! I'm Dhanashree Balaram

I am an AI Research Engineer and Project Lead, specializing in researching, building, and analyzing models and addressing challenges in 3D generative AI, vision-language models, and robotics. My role encompasses both theoretical and applied aspects of artificial intelligence. When I say "building," I refer to the process of tackling real-world problems by evaluating existing solutions and determining their applicability (applied AI). This involves critically analyzing the limitations and gaps in current methods and proposing innovative approaches to address them. I design and implement novel architectures, specialized loss functions, and algorithms tailored to the problem at hand. Additionally, I ensure these solutions are grounded in strong theoretical principles by providing theoretical guarantees and proofs based on assumptions and constraints specific to the given data or domain. In essence, my work bridges the gap between theoretical AI research and its practical application, aiming to develop cutting-edge, efficient, and robust solutions to complex problems.

Outside my professional role, I am dedicated to applying advanced machine learning techniques to address critical global challenges. This includes developing computer vision models to automate and optimize waste management systems and leveraging data science to analyze and improve education in developing countries. Specifically, I focus on studying varied teaching pedagogies to identify data-driven strategies that enhance educational outcomes and accessibility. I am driven by the belief that technology, when applied thoughtfully, can create scalable and impactful solutions for pressing societal issues.
