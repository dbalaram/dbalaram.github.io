About Me

I discovered my love for linear algebra during grad school—it was like finding the cheat code to math. One day, while trying to untangle the mysteries of the Jacobian, something just clicked, and suddenly, I could see it. It felt like unlocking a superpower (minus the cape). Who knew matrices and transformations could be so exciting? What really keeps me hooked is bringing all that abstract theory to life. Building real-world models that solve problems in OEM industries, refine marketing strategies, or even help designers unleash their creativity has been incredibly fulfilling. It’s like being a magician, except instead of pulling rabbits out of hats, I’m pulling solutions out of eigenvectors.
